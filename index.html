About Me

“Every great design begins with an even better story”

Hello! I am Daniel and I am a well-organized, efficient and professional person. I have good interpersonal skills, am an excellent team worker, and am keen and very willing to learn and develop new skills. I work on every project with passion and I enjoy problem-solving.
During these 16 years of work experience, I worked as a photographer, video-photo editor, and furniture and interior designer.
The variety of work I did allow me to indirectlygain UX skills and solve complex problems.
UX / UI I graduated from Brainster Academy (2021-2022) where I learned about the design thinking process, understanding users, challenging assumptions, redefining problems, and creating innovative solutions to prototype and test. Overall everything is about good design.
