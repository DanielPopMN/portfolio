Short Summary

Young Fashion designer, based In Skopje, Macedonia. Bachelor of Fashion design, and from recently, entered postgraduate studies in Fashion design. Art lover, pattern maker … Inspired by women’s body, ballet, music, dance. A lot of projects behind, 2 independent fashion collections, a lot of group collections during the studies, several collaborations with famous pop stars in Macedonia, assistant costume designer and course trainer for clothing and sewing.
Mitko Zanov is Skopje based fashion designer. He launched the brand ZANOV in 2015. The first collection, named "The Naked Dawn", was presented at Skopje Fashion Weekend. Besides working on his brand, Zanov has experience in costume design for ballet and theater in Macedonia and the Balkans.
Zanov needs a website that will be to present his style and Zanov's designs, ie it will be like a kind of gallery, a portfolio where all previous collections will be exhibited, a description of the collections, and a part where there will be contact, ie people who are interested in certain pieces to schedule a consultation and to come on the spot in the studio Zanov.
